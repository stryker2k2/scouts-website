Bluebonnets Rock!

Our troop was founded on May 1st, 2021 in Copperas Cove. We were originally sponsered by the local newspaper but are now sponsored by the VFW in Harker Heights.

Activities and Events

We've participated in a ton of campouts, fundraisers, and hikes. We were able to spend a weekend at the horse ranch, help the Cub Scouts cross over, cook pancakes for our sponsor organization, and clean up our local cemetary!
